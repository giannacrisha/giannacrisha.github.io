Dive Below

Beneath every story is a system.

Each project begins with listening—understanding human rhythms, cultural patterns, and emotional cues. From there, I prototype loops: feedback systems that turn insights into experiences. Whether building with code, canvas, or community, my process blends anthropology, psychology, and design thinking to surface what truly connects people.

Deep Sea

Designer. Researcher. Builder of meaningful systems.

I’m a multidisciplinary student at Brandeis University majoring in Psychology, Anthropology, and Business—with minors in Legal Studies and CAST. My work explores how creativity and systems design can reshape education, wellness, and community. I lead ventures that bridge storytelling and technology, from LifeLoops to CYCLE, while serving as a Marketing & Startup Champion at Brandeis Innovation.
