*Temporal Liberation* is a collaborative mural made with artist Claudia Bernardi and 13 Brandeis University students from diverse backgrounds, displayed in the Shapiro Campus Center atrium.

The mural depicts a grieving sun and two blue figures overlooking a scarred earth, flowing rivers, mountains, and fractal skies representing hope — alongside a bloodred abyss with anonymous figures marching toward the sun. It conveys that social situations, oppressive and liberating, are our own creations, and calls for action with intention, hope, and solidarity.
