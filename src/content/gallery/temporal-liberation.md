The mural depicts a grieving sun and two blue figures overlooking a scarred earth, flowing rivers, mountains, and fractal skies representing hope — alongside a bloodred abyss with anonymous figures marching toward the sun. It conveys that social situations, oppressive and liberating, are our own creations, and calls for![[5.jpg]] action with intention, hope, and solidarity.

![[1.heic]]

*Temporal Liberation is a collaborative mural made with artist Claudia Bernardi and 13 Brandeis University students: Gianna Crisha Saludo, Happy Emmanuel, Lex Felton, Kaitlyn Huang, Meli Jackson, Anya L'Esperance, Zixi Lin, Mel Lord, Emily Pahuamba, Fiona Ripp, Cameron Samuels, Rachel Shpuntoff, and Emily Yao.*
